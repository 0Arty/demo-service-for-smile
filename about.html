Founding Story

The Story Behind Our Mission

Smiles for Service began with a simple truth: veterans in both the U.S. and Ukraine were waiting far too long for dental care.

As Ukrainian-American dentists, we knew we could do better. We joined forces, pooled resources, and opened our clinics to veterans at no cost.
