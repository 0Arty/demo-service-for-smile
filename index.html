Our promise to veterans

When you walk into a Smiles for Service clinic, you are more than a patient—you are family. You won’t face endless paperwork or waitlists. You’ll be welcomed, listened to, and treated with the respect you’ve earned through your service.

What to include in your sponsorship/donation packages

Align the benefits with the company’s goals

Show how your partnership supports what companies care about most — brand visibility, employee engagement, and building a positive community presence.

Source: National Council of Nonprofits

Offer multiple levels of participation

Create tiered sponsorship options so companies of different sizes and budgets can choose the level that best fits their goals.

Source: Fundraising Letters Blog

Be clear about what the company receives

Clearly outline the benefits you provide — from logo placement and social media mentions to naming opportunities and special access.

Source: Charity Auctions Today

Document all the details

Put every agreement in writing to ensure both sides understand their responsibilities and expectations.

Source: National Council of Nonprofits

Leverage your unique assets

Highlight what sets your organization apart — your audience, mission, and programs — and show how companies can tap into those strengths.
